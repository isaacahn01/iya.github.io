How I Make Music

Steps

Step 1. Recording Vocals

For raw vocals, I use a program called Audacity. Audacity is a free program, but it does not have much utility as it tends to mess up original audio when adding effects and mixing. It is still good for keeping raw audio in case you need it again.

Step 2. Mixing

After spending hours recording my raw audio, I then export those files into another program called Cakewalk. In one track I would put my instrumental/beat and then start pasting my raw vocals into other tracks. Here, I play around with a lot of settings to change my voice add effects (though I'm not very good as of now :( ).

Step 3. Cleaning Up

After mixing my audio, I make one last look through and listen to my music to see if it is good enough for release. After cleaning up some of the audio, I then export the file.

Step 4. Video Production

After I'm done making my song, I put the audio into a video editor and make a quick lyric video so that I can upload it to YouTube. I typically don't add in effects here and make it very simple.

Step 5. Done!

Now that I'm done with all the necessary steps, I upload my video to my channel (Lil Shekki Music) and sit back and get feedback from some of my other friends.
